YouTube Comment Extraction and Sentiment Analysis

About the Project

YouTube comment extraction and sentiment analysis involves extracting comments from YouTube videos and analyzing their sentiment or emotional tone. Using machine learning algorithms, patterns in the text are identified to indicate positive, negative, or neutral sentiment. This information can be used for various purposes, such as understanding viewer reactions to a particular video or channel, identifying potential areas for content improvement, and targeting marketing and outreach efforts more effectively.
